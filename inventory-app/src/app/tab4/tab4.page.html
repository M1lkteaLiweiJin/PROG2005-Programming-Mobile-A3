Privacy & Security Our commitment The Inventory App is designed to help authorised staff manage stock data stored on a central REST server. We only request the minimum amount of information required to operate the service and we never share your inventory data with unrelated third parties. Data we handle

Inventory records

Item name, category, quantity, price, supplier, stock status, featured flag, and an optional note.

No personal data

The application itself does not collect user-identifiable personal information.

No device data

We do not read contacts, location, photos, or any other sensitive device-level information.

 How we keep it safe

Encrypted transport

All communication with the backend occurs over HTTPS so data in transit is encrypted.

Input validation

Strict client-side validation prevents malformed or malicious payloads from being submitted.

Minimum privilege

The app only invokes the documented REST operations and does not attempt administrative or schema changes.

Safe deletion

Destructive operations require an explicit confirmation dialog. Certain protected records (e.g. "Laptop") cannot be removed.

 Your responsibilities

Keep your device locked

Use a strong screen-lock method so unauthorised users cannot open the app on your behalf.

Verify inputs

Double-check item names before updating or deleting to avoid accidental data loss.

Report issues

If you suspect a record has been tampered with or data has leaked, contact your administrator immediately.
